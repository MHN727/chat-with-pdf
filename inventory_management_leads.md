# 🤝 UNIVERSAL FIRST MEETING STRATEGY FOR IMS

## How to Walk In and Start the Conversation

### Step 1: Visit During a BUSY Time (Smart Move)
- Visit the shop when it is **busy** — between 11 AM to 1 PM
- Watch how the owner/staff struggle to find items, check stock, or manage bills
- This is your **evidence** — you will use it later in the pitch

### Step 2: Come Back During a QUIET Time
- Return at **3-5 PM** when the owner has free time
- Say: *"Assalam u Alaikum bhai. I visited earlier and noticed your business is doing really well. Mashallah!"*
- Let them feel good first

### Step 3: Ask the Magic Pain Questions
Ask these questions one by one. **Listen carefully** to their answers:

1. *"How do you track your stock right now? Notebook or memory?"*
2. *"Have you ever run out of a popular item and lost a sale?"*
3. *"Do you know exactly how much profit you made last month — per item?"*
4. *"Has any stock ever gone missing and you couldn't figure out how?"*
5. *"When you order new stock, how do you decide what to buy and how much?"*

> [!TIP]
> **Every answer they give will be a problem.** After they answer, say: *"This is exactly what I solve. Let me show you."*

### Step 4: Open Your Laptop and Demo
- Show a **live demo** of your Inventory Management System
- Enter a sample product, add stock, sell it, show the report
- Say: *"See? In 2 clicks, you know exactly what you have, what you sold, and how much profit you made."*

### Step 5: Show the Money
- Calculate their loss:
  - *"If you lose even 1 item worth Rs.500 per day to mistakes or theft, that's Rs.15,000/month. My system costs less than that — and it STOPS the loss."*
- Calculate their gain:
  - *"If you never run out of a hot product, you make extra sales. Even 5 extra sales per day at Rs.200 profit = Rs.30,000/month extra."*

### Step 6: Leave a Proposal
- Give a **one-page printed proposal** with:
  - System features (in Urdu or simple English)
  - Pricing (one-time + monthly)
  - Your phone number and WhatsApp
