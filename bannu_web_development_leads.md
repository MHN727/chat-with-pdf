## 📋 APPROACH STRATEGY — Kaise Pitch Karein?

### 🎯 Step 1: Initial Contact (Walk-in Preferred for Bannu)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Bannu mein **in-person visit** best kaam karta hai. Phone ya email se pehle direct milein. Chai ke saath baat karein — yeh local culture hai aur trust build hota hai.

### 🎯 Step 2: Show, Don't Tell
- **Laptop ya tablet le kar jaayein** aur competitor websites dikhayein
- Bannu ke bahar similar businesses ki websites dikhayein (e.g., Peshawar ke doctor ki website)
- Unko feel karayein ke woh kitna miss kar rahe hain

### 🎯 Step 3: Pricing Strategy for Bannu Market

| Package | Price Range (PKR) | Best For |
|---------|------------------|----------|
| **Basic Landing Page** | 15,000 – 25,000 | Small shops, clinics |
| **Business Website (5-7 pages)** | 30,000 – 50,000 | Schools, hotels, restaurants |
| **E-Commerce / Booking System** | 60,000 – 100,000 | Real estate, electronics, jewelers |
| **Monthly Maintenance** | 3,000 – 5,000/month | All businesses |

### 🎯 Step 4: Closing Lines (Urdu/Pashto Context)

- *"Aap ka competitor already digital ja raha hai — aap bhi peeche nahi reh sakte"*
- *"Website aap ka 24/7 salesman hai — raat ko bhi kaam karta hai"*
- *"Hum Bannu mein hain — koi issue ho toh direct mil sakte hain"*
- *"Pehle 5 clients ko 20% discount — abhi decide karein"*
